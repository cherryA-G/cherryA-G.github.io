Projects

  • The Legend of Zelda (Unity Remaster)

    A remake of the first dungeon from the original NES game, featuring all rooms, enemies, and core mechanics. Includes a custom paintgun mechanic with a unique puzzle level accessible by pressing "4".

    Contributions: Designed and implemented the menu UI; programmed player and enemy movement, health, and damage systems; created collectables; built door lock/unlock logic; implemented pushable blocks; handled map and item placement; designed and developed the custom paintgun puzzle mechanic.

    Technologies: C#, Unity

  • The Gold Mine

    A 3D Puzzle Platform Game which restricts the player to move in a 2D movement. Includes a unique room rotation mechanic where the player can press "Z" to rotate left and "X" to rotate right.

    Contributions: All gameplay mechanics including room rotation, win celebration, player death, game timer, popup spikes, and scene changes.

    Technologies: C#, Unity
